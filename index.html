About the Open Mic

TEDx is coming to Lead City University once again! But first, we're handing the mic to five people who want to earn their place on that stage. They've been shortlisted from our list of applicants, Now they have one shot to prove their idea is TEDx-worthy This is our first-ever Open Mic Event, a space where Lead City community members can share bold ideas, personal stories, and creative performances in the TEDx format.

But this night isn't just about performance it's a proving ground. A preview of voices that could shape the conversations we'll be having next in our community Come listen. Come question. Be the Judge
